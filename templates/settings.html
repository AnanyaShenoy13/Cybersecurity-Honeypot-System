About Athena

Hi! I am Athena, a robust cybersecurity tool designed to monitor and analyze malicious activities in real-time. Our dashboard provides comprehensive insights into attempted attacks, allowing security professionals to detect, analyze, and respond to threats effectively.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Instantly track and visualize incoming attacks, including their origin and type.
  • Alert System: Receive notifications and alerts for suspicious activities, enabling swift response and mitigation.
  • Analytics: Gain deep insights with detailed analytics and visualizations, helping to identify trends and patterns in attack behaviors.
  • Settings: Customize alert preferences and dashboard configurations to suit specific security needs.

Powered by advanced technologies, Athena is your proactive defense against cyber threats, ensuring your systems remain secure and resilient.
